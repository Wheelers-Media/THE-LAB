The Upgrade Decision

Carbon vs. Ceramic Tinting

SxS cabins are open to the elements. Choosing the right film makes the difference between a hot, blinding ride and a cool, high-visibility trail experience.

Carbon Series

Baseline

A solid baseline film providing standard UV protection and a sleek dark aesthetic. Great for basic glare reduction, but offers limited heat insulation.

  • Standard UV Block (Up to 99%)
  • Standard Glare Reduction
  • Non-Reflective Black Finish

Premium Standard

Engineered with nano-ceramic particles that block solar heat where carbon fails. Crucial for open-cab environments, keeping you cool under direct sunlight and protecting dashboard screens from cracking.
