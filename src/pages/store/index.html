CCV Solutions.

Quit feeding your turbo oil vapors.

The factory CCV (Crankcase Ventilation) system routes oil vapor directly into your intake ï¿½ fouling the intercooler and building deposits on the intake manifold. Our Polar Diesel delete kits reroute or eliminate the system for cleaner air delivery and a longer-lasting turbo.
